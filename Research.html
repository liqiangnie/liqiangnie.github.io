Research Interests

Dr. Nie has co-/authored more than 150 books, journal, and conference papers. Thereinto, 100 of them are in the CCF-A venues or IEEE/ACM Trans series. His research publications have been cited around 6,000 times. He received top 2 performance of TRECVID 2010, MSRA fellowship nomination award 2012, dean's graduate research excellence award 2013, best paper nomination award of MMM 2016, best paper award of IJCAI boom 2016, and SIGMM emerging leaders in 2018. He is an AE of Information Science, an AC of ACMMM 2018/2019 and a PC chair of ICIMCS 2017. His research interests lie primarily in multimedia computing and information retrieval.
